First time going home alone, how do you feel bout that?...

So, honestly I'm a little freaked out. You know, back in high school I never really travelled to school by myself cause mom and dad always drove me there. This being my first time on the streets of Nairobi alone actually feels like Déjà vu. There's one time I had a dentist's appointment and I had to take the bus all the way to Belle view on my own. I got there safely, but I started panicking after I alighted the bus and I seriously thought I was lost. Mom was just across the road watching me, but I was so scared I couldn't see her... I did eventually and all's well that ends well, here I am today.

Here's to hoping that never recurs, especially not today. I just need to find Tom Mboya Str. How hard can that be? So help me God...
